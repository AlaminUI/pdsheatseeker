Safety Presentations from Power Distribution Systems, LLC & Brainfiller

For our international skill level and unrivaled safety presentations, Power Distribution Solutions, LLC proudly brings to the table a privileged 15-year partnership with Brainfiller Forum founder and international teaching expert Mr. Jim Phillips PE.

We cultivate this extraordinary relationship for our customers as means of designing, presenting and implementing an electrical safety training program with top of the line experience.  The in-depth references that assist our clients in understanding electrical properties, service testing, electrical personal protective equipment and analysis performance standards from a global perspective basis are nothing short of priceless.

Our team believes personnel safety is an action statement. We were the first infrared services provider in our local area to offer and implement NFPA70E safety standards to protect our technicians and customers.  Today we seek to engage your project with a “safety first” program combining our operating experience with your facility safety programs.

We know the means of sound solutions and dependability will be implemented.

Our safety presentations include the following:

  • NFPA70E Standard for Electrical Safety in the Workplace
  • Electrically Qualified Person/Basics
  • Training/Brainfiller

If the power to electrical equipment is not grounded, the grounding path has been broken, or there are live parts or bare wires, a fault current can travel through someone. Even when a piece of equipment or a tool is properly grounded, it can instantly change from safe to hazardous because of extreme conditions or rough treatment. So, we make it our mission to make sure that your employees know what hazards to watch out for and how they can protect themselves. Our safety presentations will teach employees how to work with and around electricity safely to avoid safety hazards that an cause injuries.
